Pizza

Description

This is created using dough and flour molded/flattened

Once the dough is done you can add sauce, cheese, and Pepperoni to the top

Then place in the oven and cook at 320 deg for 20minutes

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. kneed dough with flour and flatten
  2. Add above Ingredients ontop of the dough
  3. Place in oven for 20min on 320 deg
